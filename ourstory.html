Our Story

Way back in the year 19,875 BCE Burlywood National Park was founded by our creator Burly Sr. Wood. Our National Park consists of the Burlywood mountain, the Burlywood forest, Burlywood lake, and Burlywood campground. Campers can enjoy our delectable (and now mostly edible.) menu items! Take a tour on our historical Burlywood trail (Maintenance required. Temporarily shut down.) or enjoy a day at Burlywood lake where there are plenty of Burlywood fish and activities that are fun for the whole family!
